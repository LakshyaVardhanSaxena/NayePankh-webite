Who We Are

About NayePankh Foundation

NayePankh — meaning "New Wings" in Hindi — is a registered NGO founded in 2018 in Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh. We partner with communities, local governments, and passionate volunteers to create sustainable, grassroots-level change.

6+ Years of Impact

We believe that sustainable change begins at the community level. Since 2018, we've been building programs that tackle the root causes of poverty — lack of education, healthcare access, and economic opportunity.

🎯

Mission

To provide quality education, healthcare, and vocational skills to underprivileged children, women, and families — enabling them to build self-reliant, dignified lives.

🔭

Vision

A society where every young person has equal opportunity to learn, grow, and contribute — regardless of their economic background, caste, or gender.

💎

Values

Transparency, inclusion, and community-led action. We work with communities, not just for them — because lasting change comes from within.

Our Initiatives

Six pillars of action, one shared goal — a stronger, more equitable community for all.

Education

Free tuition centres, school supply drives, and digital literacy camps for first-generation learners across 12 villages.

Core Program

Healthcare

Mobile health camps, vaccination awareness drives, and partnerships with clinics to serve remote communities effectively.

Outreach

Women Empowerment

Self-help groups, legal awareness workshops, and microfinance linkages helping women build businesses and reclaim rights.

Flagship

Skill Development

Vocational training in tailoring, computer basics, and allied trades to improve employability and livelihood outcomes.

Livelihood

Community Support

Food distribution, disaster relief coordination, and peer-support networks in underserved localities throughout the region.

Relief

Hygiene Awareness

Sanitation drives, menstrual health education, and clean-water access campaigns in rural pockets of Uttar Pradesh.
